Mouse Ear

2020

I looked around my apartment and noticed I had about a dozen Mickey Ears laying around. I bought these ears to wear in the parks and show my love for Disney, but once I got home the just sat in the corner until my next trip. I decided I wanted a way to display them, so I created these wall mounted Ear Holders. Etsy Page https://www.etsy.com/shop/mrdesignsandfinds

Page Holder

2021

I always hated laying on my side while trying to read a book. I designed and 3D printed this simple page holder so anyone can hold their book open with one hand. Etsy Page https://www.etsy.com/shop/mrdesignsandfinds
